# Security Policy

## Reporting as Vulnerability

> ⚠️ **CRITICAL:** Please do not use the standard issue tracking system or standard "Bug Report" templates for security bugs. This ensures flaws are not made public before patches are ready.

### How to Submit a Report
1. On GitHub, navigate to the main page of the repository.
2. Click the **[Security](../../security)** tab.
3. On the left sidebar, under "Reporting", click **[Vulnerability reporting](../../security/advisories/new)**.
4. Click **Report a vulnerability** to open the private draft form.
5. Provide a detailed summary, including step-by-step replication instructions and the potential impact.

### Our Process
* **Acknowledgment:** You will receive a response within 48 hours.
* **Coordination:** We will collaborate with you privately to fix the flaw using a temporary private fork.
* **Disclosure:** Once a fix is released, a public Security Advisory will be published acknowledging your contribution.
